## How to Contribute

- **Found a bug?** Open an issue or drop feedback in the BHIS or TCM Discord tool channels. If it's valid it goes in `SECURITY.md`, gets fixed, and you go in this file.
- **Want to PR?** Fork the repo, branch off main, and open a pull request. Keep commits logical and describe what you changed and why.
- **Have an opinion on the output or UX?** Open an issue. Operator experience feedback is just as valuable as bug reports.

All contributors are credited in this file and in the relevant commit message.
